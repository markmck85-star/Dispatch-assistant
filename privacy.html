MCR Technical Service LLC — Dispatch Platform

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 20, 2026  |  mcrdispatch.net

Overview

MCR Technical Service LLC operates the MCR Dispatch platform at mcrdispatch.net. This platform is an internal operational tool used exclusively by MCR Technical Service LLC personnel for field service dispatch coordination, work order management, and technician notifications.

This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect information in connection with the operation of this platform.

Information We Collect

The platform collects and processes the following categories of information in the course of normal operations:

  • Work order data received via email from our service management system
  • Technician contact information (name, phone number, carrier) for dispatch notifications
  • Service location data (site name, address, state) used to route assignments
  • Dispatch activity logs including assignment timestamps and SLA tracking data

No personal information is collected from members of the public. All data processed by this platform relates to internal business operations.

How We Use Information

Information processed by this platform is used solely for the following internal business purposes:

  • Routing and assigning field service work orders to qualified technicians
  • Delivering transactional SMS and email notifications to MCR technicians regarding their assignments
  • Tracking service level agreement (SLA) compliance for open work orders
  • Maintaining dispatch records for internal reporting and operational review

We do not use this data for marketing, advertising, or any purpose unrelated to field service operations.

Email and SMS Communications

This platform sends transactional notifications via email and SMS to MCR Technical Service LLC employees and contractors. These communications are strictly operational in nature and include work order assignments, status updates, and scheduling information. Recipients are MCR personnel only. No unsolicited or marketing communications are sent through this platform.

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or share personal information with third parties for commercial purposes. Data processed by this platform may be shared with the following service providers solely to enable platform operations:

  • Email delivery providers (for transactional notification delivery)
  • Hosting and infrastructure providers (Netlify)
  • Cloud storage providers used for configuration and operational data

All third-party providers are bound by their own privacy and data handling policies.

Data Retention

Operational data is retained only as long as necessary for business purposes. Dispatch records and logs are maintained for internal review and may be purged periodically. Technician contact information is maintained only while the individual is actively associated with MCR Technical Service LLC operations.

Security

Access to this platform is restricted to authorized MCR Technical Service LLC personnel. Sensitive configuration data including API keys and credentials are stored using secure environment variable management provided by our hosting infrastructure. We apply reasonable technical safeguards appropriate to the sensitivity of the data processed.
